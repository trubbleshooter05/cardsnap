This is the mistake that costs card collectors money.
I used to just look up PSA ten prices and think, yep, send it.
But that is not the real question. The real question is: what happens if it comes back PSA nine?
CardSnap shows the raw value, PSA nine value, PSA ten value, and estimated grading costs.
This card looked profitable until I saw the PSA nine math. I almost lit the grading fee on fire.
If you grade cards, check the ROI before you submit.
