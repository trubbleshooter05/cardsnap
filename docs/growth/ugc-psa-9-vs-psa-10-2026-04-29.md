## 3. Calm: "Here's the math I check before grading."

**Hook**
Here is the math I check before grading.

**Script**
Before I submit a card, I do not start with the PSA 10 price anymore.

I start with the downside.

What is it worth raw?

What is it worth in PSA 9?

What is the PSA 10 upside?

Then I subtract grading fees, shipping, and the chance that it does not gem.

CardSnap puts that math in one place and gives me a grade-or-skip verdict.

If PSA 9 loses money, I usually hold or sell raw.
